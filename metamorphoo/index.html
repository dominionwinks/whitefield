Metamorphoō Boot Camp

We know that the outpouring of the Spirit will be upon our sons and daughters, and our young men will prophesy.

The Metamorphoō Nation is a vision to build generations of young people, godly children and teenagers, who are preserved for God in these times and are devoted to His cause. They will not bend to the pressures of their day, but will stand like Daniel and declare God's wisdom.

At the Metamorphoō Boot Camp every year, we gather these young people from all our Communities for a time of exposure to God's Word and His Spirit. Here, we see these young lives reshaped: addictions are broken, hearts are mended, scars of trauma and abuse healed, and hard hearts permeated with light.

At the Boot Camp, teenagers and children are equipped to stand for God among their peers, and to walk in His purpose for their lives.

We have seen and are persuaded that God is redeeming the next generation to Himself.

(The Metamorphoō Boot Camp ) For the promise is unto you, and to your children...
