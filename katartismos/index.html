Katartismos means an equipping, a complete furnishing. As a verb, it is to prepare and complete, to mend and repair, to make fit, to adjust, to restore, to put in order.

Katartismos Congress is a Feast of God, a Solemn Assembly, a Holy Convocation.
At this Feast, we see God do His work of creation and restoration among us. He mends broken lives and makes them whole. He gives order and shape to where there's confusion and chaos. He restores purposes and redefines destinies. He revives cold and dead altars. He takes imperfect men and furnishes them for His Glory.

At this Feast, everyone partakes and no one is left out. There's revelation and insight for all via the Teaching of the Word. There's participation in all the Gifts of the Holy Ghost for all. The Power of Jesus is available to heal and restore, and to meet every need. There's recognition and impartation of Gifts and Ministries. There's the evident Glory of God and mighty demonstrations of the Spirit and the miraculous. There's abundant Grace for greater exploits: Ministries, Ministers and Workers are equipped and repaired and furnished for much more work in the coming year.

We close every year with this Feast. We gather to take our fill of God's Glory and Power, as He works His perfection in us. It is always Heaven on earth!

KATARTISMOS CONGRESS ...where everyone and everything is as they ought to be!
